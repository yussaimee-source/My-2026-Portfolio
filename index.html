Process & Thinking

How I Solve Design Problems

Senior designers are hired for process, not just output. Two case studies — brief to measurable outcome.

01
Finance · Publishing
Scaling TAB Global's Design System Across 4 International Platforms
BriefConsistent visual identity across LinkedIn, WeChat, Instagram and email for 4 publishing brands with different tones and global C-suite audiences.
Challenge50–80 posts/month, one designer, zero template system. Brand inconsistency eroding credibility with senior banking professionals worldwide.
ApproachAudited all 4 brand identities. Built modular InDesign template library with locked brand zones. Integrated Adobe Firefly for rapid, IP-safe image generation.
ResultPer-post production time cut dramatically. Visual consistency restored company-wide. System still in active use across all 4 brands.
4×
Brands unified — The Asian Banker, TABInsights, Wealth & Society, The Banking Academy

FMCG · Retail
Nutrimetics Monthly POSM Pipeline — Concept to Shelf in 7 Days
BriefMonthly full POSM suite — catalogue, packaging, brochure, displays — with aggressive deadlines and Art Director + GM sign-off every cycle.
ChallengeCoordinating across Art Director, General Manager, and external print vendors within a rigid 7-day cycle. Zero margin for error on a global premium beauty brand.
ApproachBuilt a repeatable design-to-print workflow. Created master templates with swappable photography zones. Managed product photography in-house.
ResultEvery monthly cycle delivered on time. Zero print recalls across entire tenure.
