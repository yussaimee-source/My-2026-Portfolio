Process & Thinking

Case Studies

Senior designers get hired for process, not just output. Here's the thinking behind two key projects.

01
Finance & Publishing

Scaling TAB Global's Social Design System for 4 International Platforms

  • BriefConsistent visual identity across LinkedIn, WeChat, Instagram, and email for 4 publishing brands with different tones and global C-suite audiences.
  • Challenge50–80 posts/month with a small team, zero template system, brand inconsistency damaging credibility with global finance leaders.
  • ApproachAudited all 4 brand identities, built a modular InDesign template library with locked brand zones, integrated Adobe Firefly for rapid asset generation.
  • OutcomeReduced per-post production time, improved cross-platform consistency, system adopted company-wide across all 4 brands.
4×
Brands unified — The Asian Banker, TABInsights, Wealth & Society, The Banking Academy
02
FMCG & Retail

Nutrimetics Monthly POSM Pipeline — Concept to Shelf in 7 Days

  • BriefFull POSM suite monthly — catalogue, packaging, brochure, displays — with aggressive deadlines and senior stakeholder sign-off required.
  • ChallengeCo-ordinating across Art Director, General Manager, and external printers within a rigid 7-day cycle for a global beauty brand.
  • ApproachEstablished a repeatable design-to-print workflow, created master templates with swappable photography zones, managed product photography in-house.
  • OutcomeConsistent on-time POSM launches across all monthly promotional periods with zero print recalls during entire tenure.
