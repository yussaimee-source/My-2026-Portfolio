003 / PROCESS

HOW I THINK

Senior designers are hired for process. Here are two projects stripped back to brief → problem → outcome.

CS/01 FINANCE · PUBLISHING

Scaling TAB Global's Design System Across 4 International Platforms

BRIEF
Consistent visual identity across LinkedIn, WeChat, Instagram and email for 4 publishing brands — each with different tones and global C-suite audiences.
CHALLENGE
50–80 posts per month, one designer, zero template system — brand inconsistency was eroding credibility with senior banking professionals worldwide.
APPROACH
Audited all 4 brand identities. Built modular InDesign template library with locked brand zones and flexible content areas. Integrated Adobe Firefly for rapid, IP-safe image generation.
RESULT
Per-post production time cut dramatically. Visual consistency restored company-wide. System still in use across all 4 platforms.
4× Brands unified under one modular system
CS/02 FMCG · RETAIL

Nutrimetics Monthly POSM Pipeline — Shelf-Ready in 7 Days

BRIEF
Monthly full POSM suite — catalogue, packaging, brochure, in-store displays — always under aggressive deadlines with Art Director + GM sign-off required.
CHALLENGE
Coordinating across Art Director, General Manager, and external print vendors within a rigid 7-day cycle. Zero margin for error on a global beauty brand.
APPROACH
Built a repeatable design-to-print workflow. Created master templates with swappable product photography zones. Brought photography in-house to eliminate external delays.
RESULT
Every monthly POSM cycle delivered on time. Zero print recalls across entire tenure — the strongest quality-control record possible in high-volume FMCG production.
0 Print recalls across full tenure
