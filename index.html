003 / PROCESS

HOW I THINK

Senior designers are hired for process, not just output. Two projects — brief to outcome.

CS/01FINANCE · PUBLISHING

Scaling TAB Global's Design System Across 4 International Platforms

BRIEF
Consistent visual identity across LinkedIn, WeChat, Instagram and email for 4 publishing brands — each with different tones and global C-suite audiences.
CHALLENGE
50–80 posts/month, one designer, zero template system. Brand inconsistency eroding credibility with senior banking professionals worldwide.
APPROACH
Audited all 4 brand identities. Built modular InDesign template library with locked brand zones. Integrated Adobe Firefly for rapid IP-safe image generation.
RESULT
Per-post production time cut dramatically. Visual consistency restored. System adopted company-wide across all 4 brands and still in use.
